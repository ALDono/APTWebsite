Ingredients for a successful website

Design

At the end of the day, you want to walk away with a website that you’re proud to show off to the world. It’s important to have a modern design that matches your vision, and at the same time creates a great user experience.

User Experience

It’s impossible to overstate the importance of user experience (UX). Great UX brings people back, bad UX drives them away. Those people continuing to use your site will ultimately lead to better visibility and better engagement

Visibility (SEO)

Great SEO brings organic (free!) traffic to your website. For a website, this simply means ensuring that each page is clear, built in a structured way that both search engines and human users can understand and navigate
